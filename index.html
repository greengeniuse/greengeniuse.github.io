Welcome to GreenGenius Enviro Solutions

GreenGenius Enviro Solutions is the manufacturer and supplier of a broad range of high-performance monitoring equipment that includes Online continuous emission monitoring systems, Online water analyzers, Groundwater level recorders, and weather monitoring stations. Our customized monitoring solutions help simulate real-life environments to monitor and validate the quality and reliability of products.

We have become pioneers in delivering comprehensive monitoring capabilities with accuracy and reliability, serving key industries like defense, aerospace, automotive, and telecommunications. We strive to achieve excellence in all operations.

Products

Online Water Analyzer

Online Water Analyzer based on UV absorption, with applications in process industries. Uses a Xenon flash lamp for long-term measurement every minute. Includes physic-chemical measurements like pH, ORP, dissolved oxygen, and conductivity with external probes.

Water Level Recorder

Digital Water Level Recorder provides highly accurate water level measurements for various applications, including harsh environments. Features submersible pressure transducers with dynamic pressure compensation for accuracy over a wide measuring range.

Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System

A flexible air quality monitoring solution that supports 30+ gases and particle sensors. Ideal for environmental monitoring with applications in industrial and personal uses, it measures and monitors both gaseous and particulate pollutants.

Weather Station

Equipped with sensors to measure temperature, humidity, pressure, wind speed, precipitation, and solar radiation. Provides real-time and historical weather data for applications in forecasting, agriculture, and emergency management.

Vortex Flowmeter

Measures flow rate of liquids, gases, and steam using the Von Karman vortex principle. Commonly used in industrial processes for accurate flow measurements. Features a bluff body, also known as a shedder bar, in the flow stream.

Digital Data Logger

Utilizes IoT technology to connect sensors and devices for data collection and communication over networks. Devices are connected to networks and can be individually addressed for monitoring and analysis purposes.

Electromagnetic Flow Meter

Based on Faraday's Law of Electromagnetic Induction, this meter generates an electric field as conductive liquids pass through, measuring the flow rate proportional to the liquid's velocity.

Sound Level Meter

Also known as a noise level meter, it measures sound intensity in various environments. Common in occupational safety and environmental monitoring, it uses decibels (dB) to express sound pressure levels relative to a reference level.

Flow Discharge Radar System

Comprises two radars measuring the surface velocity and vertical height of river flow. Used for calculating discharge or volume flow rate in m³/second, combining surface velocity and river depth measurements.

Water Level Indicator

Measures water levels with options for handheld and portable devices. The measurement range indicates the depth that can be accurately assessed, essential for choosing specific applications.

Continuous Emission Monitoring System

Monitors and reports industrial air emissions, measuring pollutants like SO₂, NOx, CO, and particulate matter. Helps industries comply with environmental regulations and provides real-time air quality data for decision-making.

Triboelectric SPM

Uses the Triboflow principle for opacity-based monitoring in stacks and chimneys. The system detects flow rate without lenses, reducing maintenance needs and ensuring continuous operation.
